Our Story & Structure

The history, mission, and people behind canopy aviation in Cyprus.

01

History of Paramotor in Cyprus

Powered paragliding arrived in Cyprus through a small group of pioneer pilots in the early 2000s. The discipline has steadily grown thanks to expanding coastal sites, dedicated instructors and a tight-knit community of enthusiasts.

02

History of Paragliding in Cyprus

Free-flight paragliding in Cyprus traces back to the late 1980s, with the island's varied terrain offering exceptional coastal and mountain flying. Decades of pilots have explored sites from Troodos to the Akamas peninsula.

03

History of CYPPA

The Cyprus Paragliding and Paramotor Association was founded to unify the community, advocate for pilots' rights, and serve as the recognised national body for canopy aviation in Cyprus.

04

Mission Statement

To promote safety, develop pilots, advocate for the canopy aviation community, support competition, and act as the regulatory voice of pilots before the Civil Aviation Authority.

05

Association Structure

CYPPA operates through an elected Board, supported by specialised committees covering Safety, Training & Education, Competitions, Airspace, and Member Services.
